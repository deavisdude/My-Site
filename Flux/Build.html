Flux is a game that I made at the 2013 HHS Game Jam at Southern Polytechnic State University in Atlanta, GA. Flux was one of the five winning submissions. This game is about HIV prevention. You play as a nanobot, which is powered by T-Cells, fighting its way through a body that has been exposed to HIV. This is a bullet hell game in which the bullets are HIVs spawned by early and late-stage infected cells, foreign particles along with a couple of bosses. In the store between levels you can buy: combination ARTs, an NRTI, and extra T-Cells to power your nanobot. This game is meant to educate people about HIV, Enjoy!
