Unity Web Player | Lit Project

DISCLAIMER: This is not meant to be a finished game. In fact, it isn't even a game, really. It is really just an interactive experience. I do not own a lot of the assets, but I think it is ok because who really cares about this small thing that isn't even commercial. Please do not take this as a sample for the quality of my games, this is simply to illustrate a point for my lit class and I probably put less than ten hours into this. Now, if you are still reading and are interested in what I was trying to communicate with this "game", here you go: In this interactive experience you travel through 3 different literary periods (rationalism, romanticism, and dark romanticism). The idea is to convey the essence of each style/era through a non-traditional medium (games) and to show a connection to modern society. The connection to modern society is most prevalent during the dark romanticism phase, but can also be sensed in the rationalism phase. Honestly, the only connection in the romanticism phase is that we still have nature and bowling (sadly no mythical dwarves).
